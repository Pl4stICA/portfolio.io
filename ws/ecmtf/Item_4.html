Protoform Optimus Prime

Protoforms are Cybertronians broken down into their most basic form: an blank slate comprised of raw, free-flowing living metal. In many universes, a protoform is the first stage in a Transformer's life cycle. These protoforms only await a life-giving spark and the alt-form information necessary to transform them into fully functional, living Transformer beings. Cybertronians can often return to their protoform state for various purposes, shedding their current alternate mode. Assuming protoform is common especially on intergalactic journeys, travelling within stasis pods or by a specialized transition form.
